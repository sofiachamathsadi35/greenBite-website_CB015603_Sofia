Avocado Toast - Breakfast

A slice of toast topped with avocado was healthy never tasted so good.

×

Avocado Toast

Ingredients:
1. 1 ripe avocado
2. 2 slices of bread
3. 1 teaspoon lemon or lime juice
4. Add toppings such as cherry tomatoes, red chili flakes, olive oil, boiled egg, and cheese slice.

Steps:
1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
2. Cut the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh.
3. Mash the avocado in a bowl with lemon/lime juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted bread.
5. Add the toppings and enjoy!

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 220 to 240 kcal
Carbs 24g
Fat 12g
Protein 6g

Fruit Salad - Dinner

A bowl of colorful fruits is a rainbow you can eat.

×

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:
1. 1 chopped apple
2. 1 sliced banana
3. 1/2 cup of halved grapes
4. 1/2 cup of chopped strawberries
5. 1/2 segmented oranges
6. 1 teaspoon of honey

Steps:
1. Wash and chop all the fruits into bite-sized pieces.
2. Mix all fruits in a bowl.
3. Drizzle honey.
4. Chill for 10 to 15 minutes.

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 120 to 140 kcal
Carbs 34g
Fat 0.5g
Protein 2g

Watermelon Mojito

Ingredients:
1. 1 cup fresh watermelon
2. 8 or 10 fresh mint leaves
3. 2 teaspoons sugar
4. Club soda or sparkling water
5. Ice cubes
6. Watermelon slice & mint sprig

Steps:
1. Mix watermelon, mint, lime juice & sugar in a glass.
2. Fill with ice.
3. Top with club soda.
4. Decorate with mint & watermelon slice.

Chia Pudding - Breakfast

Tiny seeds, big health so chia pudding is goodness in a jar.

×

Chia Pudding

Ingredients:
1. 3 tablespoons chia seeds
2. 1 cup of milk
3. 1 teaspoon of honey or maple syrup
4. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
5. Berries, grapes, banans, nuts, and almonds

Steps:
1. Mix chia seeds, milk, and honey in a bowl.
2. Cover and put in the fridge for 4 hours.
3. Stir once after 2 hours.
4. Add berries, grapes, bananas, nuts, almonds and enjoy!

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 200 to 220 kcal
Carbs 18g
Fat 10g
Protein 6g

Grilled Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:
1. 1 tortilla
2. 100g of sliced grilled chicken
3. Lettuce, 1 sliced tomato, sliced cucumber, and sliced bell peppers
4. 2 cheese slices
5. 1 or 2 tablespoon of mayonnaise
6. Salt & pepper

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 350 to 400 kcal
Carbs 35g
Fat 12g
Protein 28g

Steps:
1. Lay the tortilla flat.
2. Spread mayo on it.
3. Place lettuce, tomato, cucumber, bell peppers, and grilled chicken on top.
4. Add 2 cheese slices.
5. Sprinkle salt & pepper.
6. Roll the tortilla tightly.

Banana Smoothie - Drinks

Smooth, sweet, and full of energy banana happiness in a glass.

×

Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:
1. 1 ripe banana
2. 1 cup of milk
3. 1/2 cup of yogurt
4. 1 teaspoon of honey
5. A few ice cubes

Steps:
1. Peel the banana and cut it into small pieces.
2. Put the banana, milk, and yogurt into a blender.
3. Add honey and ice cubes.
4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
5. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 180 to 200 kcal
Carbs 38g
Fat 2.5g
Protein 6g

“Soft, sweet, and a little bit wholesome — banana oatmeal cookies are comfort in every bite.”

×

Banana Oatmeal Cookie

Ingredients:
1. 2 ripe bananas (mashed)
2. 1 cup of oats
3. 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
2. Mix mashed bananas and oats.
3. Add chocolate chips.
4. Scoop onto a baking tray, flatten slightly.
5. Bake 12–15 mins until golden.

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 95 kcal
Carbs 19g
Fat 2.5g
Protein 2g

Quinoa Salad Bowl - Lunch

“A bowl of quinoa salad: light, fresh, and full of energy.”

×

Quinoa Salad Bowl

Ingredients:
1. 1 cup of quinoa
2. 2 cups of water
3. 1 cucumber, 1 cup cherry tomatoes, 1 bell pepper, 1/4 cup onion
4. 1/4 cup of parsley
5. 1/4 cup of olive oil, 2 tbsp lemon juice, salt & pepper

Steps:
1. Rinse and cook quinoa in water for about 15 minutes until fluffy, then let it cool.
2. Fluff quinoa with a fork.
3. Chop cucumber, tomatoes, bell pepper, onion, and parsley.
4. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for the dressing.
5. Combine quinoa, veggies, and dressing

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 320 kcal
Carbs 42g
Fat 13g
Protein 9g
